This database application allows managers to view all of their respective artists and perform queries to display information about artists' setlists and songs. The application also enables managers to perform queries to reveal how the artists, setlists, and songs are organized into certain venues for shows, as well as how the venue seat occupancies interact with their respective ticket holders.
